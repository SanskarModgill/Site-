Welcome to Space Facts

A website dedicated to sharing interesting facts and information about our universe — from planets to black holes.

About This Website

This website was created as a Web Programming course project. The goal is to present interesting space facts in a simple and easy-to-read way.

Space has always been fascinating to me. The universe is incredibly large and there is still so much we don't know about it. I wanted to build a site that makes learning about space fun for everyone.

The site is built using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks or libraries were used.

Explore Space Topics

Here are some of the most interesting things in our universe.

🪐

Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is known for its beautiful ring system made of ice and rock.

🌑

Black Holes

A black hole is a region in space where gravity is so strong that nothing — not even light — can escape from it.

⭐

Stars

Stars are giant balls of hot gas. Our Sun is a star. There are billions of stars in the Milky Way alone.

🌌

Galaxies

A galaxy is a system of millions or billions of stars, gas, and dust held together by gravity.

🌠

Meteor Showers

Meteor showers happen when Earth passes through debris left by comets. They can be seen with the naked eye.

🚀

Space Exploration

Humans first landed on the Moon in 1969. Today, we are working on missions to Mars and beyond.
