Explore the Universe

// cosmic wonders await

🌌

Milky Way Galaxy

Our home galaxy spans over 100,000 light-years and contains between 100–400 billion stars — including our very own Sun.

🪐

Saturn's Rings

Saturn's iconic ring system stretches up to 282,000 km from the planet, made mostly of ice particles and rocky debris.

⭐

Stellar Nurseries

Nebulae are the birthplaces of stars — vast clouds of gas and dust where gravity causes matter to collapse and ignite.

🌑

Black Holes

So dense that not even light can escape, black holes warp spacetime itself. Supermassive ones lurk at the heart of most galaxies.

🌠

Meteor Showers

When Earth passes through comet debris trails, we're treated to spectacular showers like the Perseids and Leonids.

☀️

Our Sun

A medium-sized star 109 times wider than Earth, the Sun contains 99.86% of the Solar System's total mass.
